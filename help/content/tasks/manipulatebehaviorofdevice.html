Manipulate the Behavior of a Device

In order to change the behavior of a motor axes or detector channel follow these steps:

  1. Select the device you want to change (left click) in the appropriate table (motor axes / detector channels table) in the scan module view.
  2. Whether you selected a motor axes or detector channel the corresponding view (Motor Axis View or Detector Channel View) will show the current settings of the device.
  3. Now you can enter the behavior of the device.

To get more information about the effects of these settings consult motor axis behavior and detector channel behavior .
