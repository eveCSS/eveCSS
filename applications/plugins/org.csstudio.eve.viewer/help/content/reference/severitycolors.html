(Device Inspector) Colors

The table below shows the colors used in the tables of the Device Inspector View.

ColorBoundarySeverity
grayvalue undefinedINVALID
redvalue > .HIHI || value < .LOLOMAJOR
yellowvalue > .HIGH || value < .LOWMINOR
greennone of above conditions is trueNO_ALARM
