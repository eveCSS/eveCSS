Plot View

The Plot View is the view to see the measured data of the running scan. In the left side you see the online plot and on the right the results.

Online Plot

In the OnlinePlot area you see the state of the engine. On the left side is the y-Axis1 range an on the right side the y-Axis2 range.

initialize of the plot

The plot will be initialized, if the Preinit Window Button is selected.

When the Preinit Window Button is off the plot shows the points of more than one scan Module if possible. The Table shows different scans and their effects in the plot

Minimum

The Minimum is the minimum of the y-Axis values in the plot. The x-Axis position is the associated position of the y-Axis value.

Maximum

The Maximum is the maximum of the y-Axis values in the plot. The x-Axis position is the associated position of the y-Axis value.

Center

The Center is the center position of the x-Axis and their associated y-Axis value.

Edge

The Edge is the edge position of the x-Axis and their associated y-Axis value.

Average

The Average is the average of the y-Axis values in the plot.

Deviation

The Deviation is the deviation of the y-Axis values in the plot.

FWHM

The FWHM is the full width half maximum of the y-Axis values in the plot.
